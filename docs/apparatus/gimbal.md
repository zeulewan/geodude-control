# 3-Axis Gimbal

The gimbal holds the "defunct" satellite model and provides 3 degrees of rotational freedom (pitch, yaw, roll) to simulate satellite tumble. Each axis is driven by a stepper motor through a thrust bearing.

---

## Axes

| Axis | Motor | Bearing | Notes |
|------|-------|---------|-------|
| Yaw (base) | Stepper motor | Primary roller bearing (Aidan M) | Base rotation, sits on the roller bearing |
| Pitch | Stepper motor | 80mm thrust bearing | |
| Roll | Stepper motor | 80mm thrust bearing | |

The base axis also houses the belt motor that drives the linear rail approach.
